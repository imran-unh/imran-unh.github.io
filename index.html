I am a researcher focusing on glacial lake dynamics under a changing climate, factors controlling the formation and seasonal evolution of supraglacial lakes, and glacier-lake-climate interactions. My work combines satellite remote sensing, geospatial analysis, and hazard modeling to improve understanding of outburst flood risks in high-mountain environments.
