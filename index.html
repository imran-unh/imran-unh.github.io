Imran Khan Ph.D.

Cryosphere • Glaciers and Glacial Lakes • Remote Sensing

I am a researcher focusing on long-term as well as seasonal dynamics of glacial lakes, factors controlling the formation and seasonal evolution of supraglacial lakes, and glacier-lake-climate interactions. My work combines satellite remote sensing, geospatial analysis, and hazard modeling to improve understanding of outburst flood risks in high-mountain environments.
