I study glacial lake dynamics and GLOF triggers in a changing climate, factors controlling the formation and seasonal evolution of supraglacial lakes, and glacier-lake-climate interactions. My work combines satellite remote sensing, geospatial analysis, and hazard modeling to improve understanding of outburst flood risks in high-mountain environments.

Research Interests

  • Remote sensing of glacial lakes
  • Seasonal dynamics of supraglacial lakes
  • Glacial lake outburst floods (GLOFs)
  • Glacier-lake-climate interactions
  • Multi-sensor fusion and geospatial analytics for hazard assessment
