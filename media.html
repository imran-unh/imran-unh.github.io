Media & Outreach

Talks, competitions, and outreach related to cryosphere research, glacial lakes, and climate hazards.

March 2025

Three Minute Thesis (3MT) Competition – University of New Hampshire

Presented my Ph.D. research on glacial lake monitoring and cryosphere hazards in a 3-minute research communication format, highlighting the societal importance of monitoring glacial lakes under climate change.

December 2024

Purdue University – Superheroes of Science

Talked about my research on glacial lakes, remote sensing, and why monitoring cryosphere hazards is critical for climate resilience and downstream communities.

December 2023

New Hampshire Social Venture Innovation Challenge – GLOFWatch

Presented GLOFWatch, an idea integrating satellite imagery and weather data to provide early warning for glacial lake outburst floods (GLOFs). The project received the Most Impact Potential Award.
